Deep learning salary predictor trained on 260K US Gov  H1B records. 
Engineered 82 features from raw government data including rule-based 
job title categorization and log1p transformation for skewed wage distributions. 
Error analysis revealed model underestimates high earners above $200K due to 
missing years of experience data.
